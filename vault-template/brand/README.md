# Brand Foundation

Static files the agents read before producing any wiki entry or synthesis note.
You edit these. Agents never rewrite them.

## Files to create
- `voice.md` — how you sound (tone, rhythm, sentence length)
- `style.md` — formatting rules (em dashes? emojis? heading levels?)
- `banned-words.md` — words and phrases that flag as AI slop
- `positioning.md` — what you stand for, what you reject
- `audience.md` — who consumes your content, what they want

## Why this matters
Without brand foundation, wiki entries come out in generic AI voice.
With it, your wiki sounds like you wrote it, even when an LLM wrote it.

See `~/SecondBrain/brand/` on a working system for examples.
